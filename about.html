About Us

Pictoriala Inc., one of Cambodia's leading commercial printing companies, is well known for its high quality offset printing and manufacturing of paper packaging and security documents. We bring many years of experiences to the world of modern printing and packaging.

Our printing section features large, high-end printing machines, including 6-color offset pesses with coaters

Our production line is equipped with high quality Diecut machines and supporting equipment such as computerized trimming machine, perfect binding and stitching lines, laminating machine, numbering and perforate numbering machines.

Our succuss rests on a basis of good business sense and dedication to quality which has kept us on a path of continuing growth and innovation. Our high quality, dependable printing has made us a valued supplier to both local and overseas customers.

We are proud to work with large-scale companies and organizations including ACLEDA Bank, Post Bank, RMA, EFG, British American Tobacco (BAT), Ministry of Health, Ministry of Education, AMRET and VisionFund Cambodia.
