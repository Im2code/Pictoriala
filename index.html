Who are We

NetPrint is a local printing company based Cambodia, Phnom Penh. Our company provide a range of products from Pharmaceutical and Food Packaging to Brochures and Newspapers. As such our mission is to provide companies with quality and responsive service, localy.

Our Services

NetPrint is ideally positioned to fufill all your printed packaging needs, from start to finish line. Our facilities include prepress, press and postpress. We offer extensive prepress services including color separation and image setting. By providing these services on the same premises as our press and postpress procedures, we are able to save time and guarantee the quality of prepress processing.

Our state-of-the-are equipment makes full use of computerized units and remote control technologies, enabling us to verify printing plate ink density and to store and transmet information to pre-adjust inking units. All this substantially reduces make ready timee and increases turnover efficiency.

Whether your order calls for highly technical specifications or for mass-consumer materials, our technological lead allows us to meet your requirements.

We provide a one stop service for all printing tasks:

  • Material preparations
  • Printing
  • Folding
  • Coating
  • Diecut
  • Binding
  • Delivering
